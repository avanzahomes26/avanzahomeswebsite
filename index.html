Simple 6-Step Process

From strategy call to passive income in 6–7 months

We've refined this process across 120+ Florida builds. You make one decision — we handle the rest.

1

Strategy Call & Agreement

Discuss your investment goals, timeline, and budget. We form a 50/50 LLC and align on the right Florida market for you.

2

Capital Investment & Lot Selection

Your $50,000+ is deployed. Avanza uses data analysis to identify the highest-yield lot in Sebring, Palm Bay, or Citrus Springs.

3

Construction Financing Secured

Avanza leverages 8+ years of banking relationships to secure 100% construction financing under its credit — not yours.

4

Hands-Off Build (6–7 months)

Weekly photo updates, live site cameras, and a dedicated project manager. You cover $2,000–$2,400/month in carry costs while we build.

5

Completion & Exit Strategy

Choose to sell (50/50 profit split), rent (50/50 cashflow), or refinance and pull equity. Recent completions have appraised $140,000+ above cost.

6

Ongoing Passive Income

Avanza manages rental operations, maintenance, and tenant relations. You collect your 50% share every month without lifting a finger.
